AI & Security Talking Points

🤖 AI & Prompting (In Plain English)

Groq: The "Turbocharger"

Think of Groq as the ultra-fast engine under the hood. While standard AI can be slow to respond, Groq processes data in milliseconds. For our users, this means compliance checks happen instantly, not in minutes.

Prompts: The "Job Description"

A prompt isn't just a question; it's a professional job description. We give the AI a 10-page expert manual on sustainability rules. This ensures that every time it looks at a company, it thinks like a senior ESG consultant, not just a chatbot.

LLM Brain (Llama-3.1)

We use the Llama-3 brain because it’s world-class at logic. It doesn't just "guess" if a company is green—it breaks down the input into structured pillars like Carbon, Waste, and Social Impact to give a grounded score.

🛡️ Security & Privacy (The "Safety First" Approach)

The "Digital Shield" (Prompt Injection)

Attackers often try to "trick" AI into giving wrong or dangerous answers. We've implemented a custom digital shield that catches these linguistic tricks before they ever reach the AI, keeping our results reliable and safe.

PII Filter: "Your Secrets are Safe"

Before any data leaves our system to be analyzed, we scrub it for private information like names or emails. We have a "Zero-Exposure" policy, meaning your sensitive corporate data stays within our secure walls.

The "Clean-In, Clean-Out" Policy

We sanitize data twice: once when it enters the system (to stop hackers) and once when the AI answers (to ensure the response doesn't contain any hidden malicious code). It's total end-to-end safety.
